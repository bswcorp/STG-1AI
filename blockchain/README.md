STG-1AI is not just an AI engine — it is a **sovereign intelligence architecture** composed of five integrated stages. Together, they form the backbone of STG’s financial and ecological sovereignty.

1. **AIGARTH (Root & Physical)**

2. **LUMEN-Sovereign (Logic & Financial)**
   - Financial oracle managing SovereignToken.sol (Unit 008).
   - Executes swaps, settlements, and liquidity injections with precision.

3. **QUORUM-SENTINEL (Defense & Compliance)**
   - Governance & compliance shield (Governance.sol).
   - Automates KYC/AML, audit trails, and regulator reporting.

4. **AURORA-Nirkabel (Connection & Speed)**
   - High-speed pulse connecting STG nodes globally.
   - Ensures millisecond execution across Dubai, Tokyo, London.

5. **SANGNAGA (Sovereign Executor)**
   - Final executor of smart contracts.
   - Executes blockchain actions once LUMEN and SENTINEL approve.
